Road Sense — 5-Minute Pitch Outline & Visuals

Bullet points only + High impact visual suggestions. Designed for a 5-minute presentation.

Slide 1 & 2: Title / Cover

  • Project: Road Sense
  • Tagline: Turning city fleets into smart infrastructure sensors.
  • Core Idea: Proactive road maintenance powered by Edge AI.
🖼️ Visual Suggestion: Sleek split-screen. Left half: A Riyadh bus driving on a road. Right half: The same road transitions into a digital mesh/wireframe with glowing red dots indicating "sensor data." Road Sense logo in the center.

Slide 3: Problem Statement

  • Reactive Maintenance is Expensive: Repairing a pothole (SAR 100-200/m²) costs 1,000% more than sealing an early crack (SAR 4-11/m).
  • Data Blind Spots: Relying on citizen complaints means damage is already done.
  • Current Tech: Specialized scanning trucks cost SAR 3.75 Million each and scan rarely.
🖼️ Visual Suggestion: A striking side-by-side photo. Left: A massive pothole destroying a car tire (Label: SAR 200/m²). Right: A tiny crack being sealed quickly (Label: SAR 10/m). Text in the middle: "Timing is everything."

Slide 4: Innovation Overview

  • The Shift: Don't buy new scanning trucks. Install low-cost IoT triggers on existing city buses and garbage trucks.
  • The Cost: ~SAR 75 per trigger unit. Continuous, daily road scanning for pennies on the dollar.
🖼️ Visual Suggestion: An icon-based equation: [Bus Icon] + [Small ESP32 Chip Icon (SAR 75)] = [Glowing City Map]. Next to a crossed-out picture of a massive, multi-million Riyal scanning truck.

Slide 5: Target Audience

  • Primary: Municipalities (Amanat) — Need live data to prioritize SAR 42B budgets effectively.
  • Secondary: Citizens & Drivers — Need safer commutes and zero rim/tire damage.
🖼️ Visual Suggestion: Two large icons. Left: A government building (Amanat) with the text "SAR 42B Budget Optimization." Right: A steering wheel with the text "Zero Tire Damage."

Slide 6: Value Proposition

  • Predictive, not Reactive: Catch road degradation before it becomes a structural failure.
  • Visual Verification: Operators get a photo of the defect *before* dispatching a crew.
  • Live Dashboard: Real-time mapping and automated severity scoring.
🖼️ Visual Suggestion: A high-quality mockup of your live web dashboard displayed inside a sleek laptop frame, showing the heatmap and the "Critical Alerts" KPIs.

Slide 7: The Prototype (Proof of Concept)

  • Hardware: ESP32-C3 + MPU6050 Accelerometer.
  • Edge AI: Algorithm filters normal driving noise and accurately triggers on true impacts.
  • Live Sync: Proves immediate transmission of severity data to the web dashboard.
🖼️ Visual Suggestion: A close-up photo of your actual breadboard/PCB setup next to a smartphone showing the live dashboard updating as you shake the sensor.

Slide 9: Market Viability

  • The Budget: KSA allocated SAR 42 Billion to infrastructure in 2025.
  • The Goal: Vision 2030 aims to rank 6th globally in road quality. Road Sense is the data engine to achieve this.
🖼️ Visual Suggestion: A massive, bold typography number: SAR 42,000,000,000. Below it, the official Vision 2030 logo and the text "Target: 6th Globally in Road Quality."

Slide 10: Business Model

  • B2G SaaS: Monthly dashboard subscription for municipalities. Ultra-low cloud costs because we only upload single photos via Wi-Fi.
  • Hardware: One-time SAR 75/unit. (SAR 375,000 to cover an entire city of 5,000 buses).
🖼️ Visual Suggestion: A 3-tier icon layout. Tier 1: Hardware (One-time SAR 75). Tier 2: SaaS (Monthly Subscription). Tier 3: Cloud Cost (A tiny Wi-Fi router icon representing zero cellular LTE costs).

Slide 11: Feasibility

  • Off-the-shelf Parts: Standard ESP32 & MPU6050 chips.
  • Zero New Vehicles: Mounts securely on existing public transport.
  • Reuses Existing Tech: Relies on the fleet's *already-installed* dashcam and GPS.
🖼️ Visual Suggestion: A photo of the inside of a bus dashboard showing how easily the small chip tucks away into the fuse box, alongside a checkmark list emphasizing "Zero New Cameras."

Slide 12: Challenges & Risks

  • Risk: Vehicle Power & Durability.
    Mitigation: 12V/24V to 5V buck converter safely tapped from the fuse box.
  • Risk: False Positives (Speed bumps).
    Mitigation: Dashcam snapshot allows human operator to visually verify the defect before sending a repair truck.
🖼️ Visual Suggestion: A split screen. Left: A photo of a speed bump. Right: A dashcam snapshot showing the speed bump, with an AI box or operator UI checking a box that says "Reject - Intentional Bump."

Slide 13: Impact & Sustainability

  • Short-term: Immediate hazard mapping to prevent accidents.
  • Long-term: Prolongs asphalt lifespan and cuts the massive carbon footprint of heavy road reconstructions.
🖼️ Visual Suggestion: A road graphic that extends to the horizon. A timeline calendar fading from 5 years to 15 years, with a green leaf icon representing the carbon savings of avoiding massive repaving machines.

Slide 14: Next Steps

  • Integration: Direct API hooks into municipal ticketing (e.g., Balady) for automated repair dispatch.
  • Predictive Timelines: AI recommendations for optimal asphalt overlay timing based on vibration trends.
